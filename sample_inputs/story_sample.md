Story: User updates customer profile
As a customer service agent, I want to update a customer profile so that contact information stays accurate.

Acceptance Criteria:
AC1: Mandatory fields name and primary phone must be provided.
AC2: Email must be unique across active customers.
AC3: Unauthorized users cannot update customer profiles.
AC4: Audit history must capture previous and new values.
AC5: Profile update should be reflected in downstream CRM sync jobs.
